Programming

Projects made during my computer science studies at University of Savoy, France

Spots Near Annecy


Professionnal website to showcase best nature spots and activities to do near Annecy.


You can choose a spot from the list, from the "top 4 spots of the month" or even load a random spot!


After selecting a spot, on it's page you'll have different information such as a description, a ranking against other spots, charts, map, photos and videos.


IsoBoat


School little C endless isometric game project where you control a boat and need to avoid obstacles.


Ater choosing a boat you can now cruise in the ocean, but keep an eye on the obstacles!


There are different obstacles for different damage hits on your boat.


Choosing a more resistant boat will cost you some money, you can earn some by avoiding as much obstacles as possible.


NightKnight


School Unity project multiplayer game where you need to survive in a blacked out castle as a knight when other players try to kill you.


Each player has a health and hunger bar, which can be both filled by walking on green and blue spheres respectively. Those spheres are randomly placed in the map.


The more you walk or run, the more you are using your hunger bar. If it gets empty your health bar will decrease.


And of course if this one gets empty you die.
